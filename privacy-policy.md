SpeedCtrl — Privacy Policy
SpeedCtrl does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All settings and per-site preferences are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.sync, which syncs across your own devices only and is never accessible to us.
No analytics, no tracking, no external servers. The extension operates entirely on your device.
If you have questions, open an issue at https://github.com/adiiace/speedctrl.
